## Production Tips

**Recording the app:**
- Use a clean browser window (no bookmarks bar, no other tabs visible)
- Upload a visually striking image — something with good color and a clear subject
- Select at least 12–15 formats so the crop grid looks impressive
- Move through the steps at a steady pace — not too fast to follow, not slow enough to bore

**Screen recording tools:**
- Mac: Command + Shift + 5 (built in)
- iPhone: Settings > Control Center > Screen Recording
- OBS (free, works on everything)

**Editing tips:**
- Keep text overlays large and readable — minimum 40pt equivalent on mobile
- Use your brand colors (indigo/white from the site) for text backgrounds
- Add subtle zoom-ins on key moments (upload, export click, ZIP opening)
- Speed up any "waiting" moments (page loads, export progress) to 2–4x

**Posting strategy:**
- Post Reel 1 (The Frustration Reel) first — it's the most relatable and shareable
- Space them 2–3 days apart
- Reels 3 and 5 work well as saved/shared content — educational and satisfying
- Reel 6 targets a different audience (freelancers/agencies) — good for expanding reach
- Always include "Link in bio" in the caption and make sure aspctratio.com/signup is your bio link

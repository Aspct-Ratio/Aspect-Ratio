## Description (Product Hunt "About" section)

ASPCT RATIO is an asset slicing tool for anyone who needs images in multiple sizes — marketers, designers, agencies, freelancers, e-commerce teams, and content creators.

**The problem:** Every campaign, product launch, or social post requires images in 10–30 different sizes. Instagram feed, Story, YouTube thumbnail, Pinterest pin, display ads, Amazon listing — each one needs different dimensions and framing. Most teams do this manually in Photoshop or Canva, one export at a time. It takes hours.

**How it works:**

1. Upload your source images (JPG, PNG, WebP)
2. Select the formats you need from 60+ built-in presets (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, Pinterest, LinkedIn, X, Amazon, Shopify, Google Display, IAB ads, and more)
3. Smart crop automatically positions your subject in every frame — drag to adjust any crop individually
4. Add text overlays (headlines, CTAs, promo codes) with full typography controls, then apply copy across all formats with one click
5. Export a single ZIP file with everything named and organized by channel, platform, and placement

No software to install. Runs entirely in the browser.

**Built for:**
- Agencies delivering multi-channel campaign assets to clients
- In-house brand teams who need to move fast without adding headcount
- Freelancers who want to deliver polished, organized asset packages
- Content creators repurposing one photo across every platform they post on
- E-commerce teams managing product images across marketplaces

We built ASPCT RATIO because we were tired of spending entire afternoons on what should be a 5-minute task. If you've ever opened the same image 15 times in Photoshop just to change the canvas size, this is for you.

## Launch Day Strategy

### Before launch day:
- [ ] Create your Product Hunt maker account at producthunt.com
- [ ] Fill out your profile completely (photo, bio, links)
- [ ] Schedule the launch for a **Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday** (highest traffic days)
- [ ] Capture all 5 screenshots above at 1270x760px
- [ ] Record a short GIF or video of the app in action
- [ ] Have 5–10 friends/colleagues ready to upvote and leave genuine comments in the first 2 hours (this matters a lot for momentum)

### Launch day:
- [ ] Post goes live at 12:01 AM PT (Product Hunt resets daily)
- [ ] Immediately post your Maker Comment (the one above)
- [ ] Share the Product Hunt link on your personal social media, LinkedIn, X/Twitter
- [ ] Respond to every comment within the first 6 hours — engagement signals to the algorithm that your product is active
- [ ] Send a personal message to anyone in your network who might upvote: "Hey, I just launched my tool on Product Hunt — would mean a lot if you checked it out and left an upvote if you think it's useful"
- [ ] Do NOT ask people to "upvote" in public posts — Product Hunt penalizes vote manipulation. Instead say "I just launched on Product Hunt, would love your feedback"

### After launch:
- [ ] Add a "Featured on Product Hunt" badge to your website (they provide an embed code)
- [ ] Follow up with everyone who commented — these are your earliest potential users
- [ ] Write a short "launch recap" post on X or LinkedIn sharing your results (views, upvotes, signups) — this content performs well and keeps momentum going
